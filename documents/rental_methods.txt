RENTMED HEALTHCARE - RENTAL METHODS INFORMATION

Thank you for your interest in RentMed Healthcare rental services.

We offer two primary rental methods to suit your needs:

1. PRIVATE RENTALS
   - Direct rental agreements with individuals
   - Flexible rental periods
   - Competitive pricing
   - Quick approval process
   - Direct billing to customer

2. MEDICAL AID SCHEME RENTALS
   - Rental through your medical aid provider
   - Coverage subject to medical aid benefits
   - Pre-authorization may be required
   - Direct billing to medical aid
   - May require co-payment depending on your plan

HOW TO PROCEED:

1. Choose your preferred rental method
2. Contact us at enquiries@rentmed.co.za
3. Provide your requirements and details
4. Receive a customized quotation
5. Complete the rental agreement
6. Arrange delivery or pickup
